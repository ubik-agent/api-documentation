## The Problem with Modern AI Integration

Organizations and developers often struggle with fragmented AI implementations that don't share knowledge or work together effectively. UBIK is designed to solve these core challenges:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Siloed Tools" icon="lock">
    AI tools often exist in isolation. UBIK provides a unified workflow where your AI sales pipeline can talk to your CRM, and your support agent can access your knowledge base seamlessly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prompting Fatigue" icon="rotate-ccw">
    AI models are stateless. UBIK introduces a working memory, so you don't have to repeat context in every interaction, allowing agents to resume where they left off.

  <Card title="Limited Context" icon="file-x">

## How UBIK Works

UBIK's core philosophy is **multistep logic**. Instead of treating AI as an unpredictable black box, we break down complex problems into smaller, manageable tasks. This dramatically improves the reliability, efficiency, and capability of our agents.

Our API gives you granular control over every aspect of your AI agents:
-   **Behavior**: Define how your agent thinks with custom logic loops.
-   **Instructions**: Shape your agent's personality and purpose.
-   **Tools**: Expand your agent's capabilities with custom skills.
-   **Knowledge**: Connect your agent to the right documents and data.
